==============================================================================

1. UNIVERSAL ACTUALIZATION

Canonical conclusion

Actualization is universal.

Actualization is NOT restricted to the Physical Realm.

Rather, it governs every ontological domain sustained by the Matrix.

Physical actualization is only one particular realization of the universal
actualizing process.

Therefore actualization governs:

• Acts of Consciousness
• Meanings
• Intentionality
• Formal Structures
• Souls
• Angelic beings
• Q-Substances
• Biological organizations

The Realm of Forms possesses genuine dynamics.

Actualization belongs equally to both realms.

------------------------------------------------------------------------------

2. TWO COMPLEMENTARY REALMS

Canonical wording

Reality consists of two complementary and inextricably interlocked domains
of lawful dynamics.

They are:

• Realm of Forms

• Physical Realm

They are neither two universes nor two disconnected substances.

They constitute one coherent ontological reality continuously sustained by
the Matrix through its actualizing potency.

------------------------------------------------------------------------------

3. MATRIX

Canonical clarification

The Matrix:

• possesses no consciousness;

• possesses no intentionality;

• possesses no agency;

• possesses no free will;

• possesses actualizing potency.

Its function is neither choosing nor creating.

Its function is to sustain the universal lawful order through which every
admissible actualization becomes actual while every incompatible
transformation remains non-actualized.

Theologically, the Matrix is continuously sustained and empowered by the
Soar of the Logos (Dialogue), while remaining itself non-conscious.

------------------------------------------------------------------------------

4. Q-FORM

Canonical refinement

Q-Form is the universal principle of instantiation.

Its function is identical in both ontological realms.

The distinction between the realms does NOT lie in Q-Form.

It lies in the participation mode of the substrate through which Q-Form
becomes instantiated.

Status

dynamics of the Realm of Forms.

Definition

The property of the Q-substrate whereby no additional ontological
organization is introduced beyond the instantiated Form itself.

The substrate contributes no ontological resistance to Form.

------------------------------------------------------------------------------

EPISTEMOLOGICAL TRANSPARENCY

Definition

The property of the Q-substrate whereby the instantiated Form is immediately
intelligible without mediation through topological organization.
